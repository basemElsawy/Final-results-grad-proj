Deadlift

The deadlift is an exercise that engages the whole body and not just your upper or lower body you need to stop thinking that deadlifts are a lower back exercise
deadlifts are one of the unique exercises that has many variations and is used by many sports to increase the strength of invidiuals it uses literally every muscle i your body starting from the placement of your toes to stiffness of your core muscles to the strength of your upper back muscles.

The main lift of the section is the deadlift.

What is the deadlift ?

Deadlifts are highly effective at increasing functional strength due to the activation of your largest lower body muscles. They also train you for the functional activity of safely lifting objects off of the floor, which is a key skill for day-to-day activities.

Is it safe to deadlift ?

Every exercise is dangerous if you did not perform it right the deadlift is one of these exercises the deadlift is an exercise that works the most important parts of the body and it needs a ton of warm up before performing

Things you should focus on when deadlifting ?!

The core muscle group is one of the largest muscle group in the human body and when braced correctly it keeps your spine stable as much as possible

Deadlift Accessories

as shown in the description and the overview you need to have a strong back muscle in order to create enough stiffness in your upper back for a good repitition deadlift.

Accessories to master your deadlift

Of course to master your deadlift you need to deadlift more but these exercises are here to strengthen the muscles you use in a deadlift

List of accessorie exercises

Pendlay-rows

Pendlay rows are really good for explosive power in pulling the bar off the ground this exercise can be performed in 2 ways the sumo stance and the conventional stance you can do this exercise with a mediocre weight and execute it in sets of 5 by 5 reps and rest for 1 min and 30 secs between sets.

Snatch grip deadlifts

Snatch grip deadlift is the perfect exercise for upper back strength works best when pulling the lats towards you and create enough stiffness in your core before performing the lift you can perform the exercise in sets of 5 by 5 reps and rest for 1 min and 30 secs between sets

Single arm dumbbell-rows

A single arm is mandatory for strength in your arm invidually and its good for building muscles in your lats and upper back you can perform this exercise in 3 or 4 sets by 10 or 12 reps and rest 30 to 45 secs between sets

Bench-Press

Bench press is the master of all the pressing exercises its the true strength test to your upper body since in advanced levels of bench pressing you start putting shear loads on your arms and your chest muscles resulting in producing muscles thickness and a massive looking shoulder and upper bench press is one of the main lifts in powerlifting it has rules to make the lift correct and also adding more weight does give you more advantage in the competition
